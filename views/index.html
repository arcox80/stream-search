Wait, what is Stream Search?

Figuring out what show or movie to watch is hard enough, but once you know what you want to watch, you then have to figure out how to watch it.

  • Is it available for rent or purchase online?
  • Is it available on a streaming service I’m already paying for?
  • Is it available on a free streaming service with commercials?

This is where Stream Search comes in. Simply enter the title of the movie or show you want to watch, and we will tell you if it can be found online and the ways you can watch it.

Ok, so what does a search show me?

When you search for the title of a series or movie you want to watch, Search Stream will show you the online viewing options you have available, if there are any. You can then choose the streaming service to which you are subscribed or purchase a viewing option from an online retailer. Just simply click on the link, and we’ll send you to the appropriate site.

So why should I register?

Well, registering allows you to use the site and create your own personal watchlist. (Please know your information will always remain private and will never be sold.) As you search for various titles that you want to watch, you can click on the “Add to Watchlist” button on the desired result, and it will be added to your watchlist. Your watchlist is displayed on your dashboard every time you log in to the site.

Anything else?

Yes, I’m glad you asked! Naturally, you will have told all of your friends about this site, and they will have all registered accounts on here as well. Maybe, you’re curious about what is in a friend’s watchlist, or you need an idea for something to watch. You can search for your friend’s name, and see what they have added to their watchlist. Then, you can simply add whatever show or movie you like to yours!
